Who We Are

Electrical products are a vital and necessary part of our lives - from power plants that provide energy, to the lighting in our homes and offices, to running entire cities. Virtually anywhere you look, you’ll see electrical products in use!

Since 1957, CED has been committed to the electrical industry, buying and selling products from the world’s foremost suppliers. Dedication to our customers, combined with a focus on long term growth within the electrical distribution industry gives us the capability for continued success and ensures our customers a continuity of service second to none!
